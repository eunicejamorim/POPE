Women’s Clothing For All Lifestyles

New seasons are the perfect time to refresh your closet with essentials for everything you do — work, play, celebrate, rest, and everything in between. And each season, Gap introduces women’s clothing to curate a wardrobe of on-trend, versatile modern classics. Spend less time thinking about what to wear to work, how to dress for a wedding, and how to style casual outfits for the weekend by getting dressed in Gap.

Looking for women’s clothes for work? Gap has office outfits that can take you from work to weekend. A blazer with a pair of jeans is forever — but take it up a notch with our best-selling faux-leather in your favorite denim fits like our ’70s Flare Jeans, Cheeky Straight Jeans, and Vintage Slim Jeans. Want a classic preppy look? Try cool corduroy jeans or comfortable khakis with a button-up shirt — the Big Shirt and the Perfect Shirt are favorites that come in enough colors, patterns, and fabrics to wear every day. Finish it off with a cozy layer, like plush sweaters, cardigans, and dusters. CashSoft sweaters have been a winter must —plush like cashmere and buttery soft. Looking to dress it up? From shirt dresses and sweater dresses to satin dresses and wrap dresses, our women’s dresses for work are perfect for any office environment.

Shop For The Occasion

No matter the time of year, it’s always party season. When you’re ready to get decked out, visit the party shop to find outfits for every event on your calendar. Think: a velvet or faux-leather mini dress with heels or boots, a sequin mini skirt leveled up with a satin shirt, a velvet halter top with a faux-leather mini skirt — the outfits options are endless.

For more casual events like date nights, try pairing your favorite flare jeans with a puff sleeve top, satin shirt, or velvet bodysuit and our Pointy Boots or Chelsea Boots. Add some statement jewelry for an extra pop. Because some of the best parties are at your own house, check out our most comfortable sweats, loungewear, and pajamas to warm up and chill all the way out.

You can choose to opt-out of sharing one or both types of information.

Step 2: To opt-out of Offline Info sharing, please fill out this Webform. If you start with Step 1, just click the Your Privacy Choices link at the bottom of our site afterwards to return and complete Step 2.
