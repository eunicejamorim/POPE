Our mission is to share and grow the world’s knowledge.

The vast majority of human knowledge is still not on the internet. Most of it is trapped in the form of experience in people’s heads, or buried in books and papers that only experts can access. More than a billion people use the internet yet only a tiny fraction contribute their knowledge to it. We want to democratize access to knowledge of all kinds—from politics to painting, cooking to coding, etymology to experiences—so if someone out there knows something, anyone else can learn it. Our mission is to share and grow the world's knowledge, and we're building a world-class team to help us achieve this mission.

What we are building

Quora is a global knowledge sharing platform with over 400 million monthly unique visitors, connecting people to share insights on various topics and providing a unique platform to learn, share, and connect with others.

Poe lets people ask questions, get instant answers, and have back-and-forth conversations with AI.

Our values

At Quora, every team member embodies these core values. We believe this makes us successful and work so synchronously together.

Awareness

We are rigorous in our decisions and candid in our communication.

Drive

We aim high and do whatever it takes to get things done.

Agility

We are nimble in our processes and systems, ready to adapt to a changing world.

Pragmatism

We stay grounded in the outcomes that truly matter for our mission.

Mission First

We place long-term, collective impact ahead of personal achievement.

Why Quora?

Visit our "Why Quora" page to read firsthand accounts from our employees about their experiences, what they appreciate most about our culture, and why they love working with us.

Remote first

All of our positions can be performed remotely and we support employment from multiple countries around the world. (For any rare exceptions that do require work from a specific location, it will be noted explicitly in the individual position's job description.)

However, because we work in highly collaborative product teams, we do value synchronous coordination and need for people to be able to talk to each other in real time. We have designated 9am to 3pm Pacific Time as "coordination hours", and most employees will be expected to be available for meetings and impromptu communication during this time, regardless of where they are located.

While we are a remote company first, we recognize that working for home isn’t for everyone. To support employees in selecting the best space for their working style and productivity, we are excited to offer the Coworking Space Program. Eligible employees can now apply for reimbursement of a coworking space or for use of a private office at a WeWork paid by Quora. Employees will receive a document outlining the eligibility requirements, how to apply, and program FAQ.

Note: our career page's integration with external job boards requires a location for each job posting. Please feel free to apply to any position if you are authorized to work in one of our employment eligible countries.

Interviewing at Quora

Our goal at Quora is to make the interview process both as friendly and as efficient as it can be. While each individual journey may vary, we strive to maintain consistency and fairness by following a similar process. Read on to learn about the different steps involved in our interview process and visit our Interviewing at Quora Space for more tips and tricks.

  • Thank you for considering a career at Quora! Our hiring teams will carefully review your application and respond as soon as possible with any next steps. In the meantime, explore our careers page, LinkedIn, or even test your interviewing skills with one of the Bots on Poe.

  • We're excited to have you in our recruitment process at Quora! One of our recruiters will be in touch soon to schedule a chat. We'll delve into your past experiences, discuss your career objectives, and make sure all of your questions are answered before you meet with the Hiring Manager. We're looking forward to getting to know you better and discovering how you could thrive in a role at Quora!

  • To allow all candidates to shine during the hiring process, we incorporate role-specific practical tasks (such as coding challenges, presentations, or mock scenarios) into our evaluation process. This will allow you to demonstrate your unique skills and expertise and ensure a more relaxed and relevant assessment experience.

    By focusing on the practical skills required for each role, we're able to identify if we're a match for each other, regardless of background or traditional credentials.

  • Our virtual onsites typically occur over one or two days. During your onsite, you will meet a wide range of interviewers from across the company in a series of interviews. This stage allows you to showcase all aspects of your skills and gives you insight on the types of things you will be working on day-to-day.

  • Following the final onsite interview, candidates who successfully progress to the next stage will have the chance to connect with their future colleagues to answer any last minute questions or concerns. This final stage of the process provides a deeper understanding of the company culture and facilitates a seamless transition into your new role should you accept our offer.

  • Welcome to Quora! We’re so excited you’ll be joining us. Leading up to your first day, you will receive some onboarding information from us to ensure you are all set up and ready to go for your first day. We can’t wait to work with you!
