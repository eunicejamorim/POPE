ScienceDirect: Celebrating 25 years of discovery

Supporting research, scholarship and discovery

For 25 years, ScienceDirect has worked with researchers and librarians to inspire and promote interdisciplinary research, scholarship and discovery. From the early days of digitizing peer-reviewed literature to our current content syndication partnerships, product integrations and a host of tools that make search and discovery fast and easy, we are honored to serve and be a partner with these research and scholarly communities.

Time-saving features

Remote access

Access anytime and from anywhere without being limited to an IP range. Simply register and then sign in to activate remote access.

Search

Sophisticated, intuitive search and retrieval tools make it easy to discover more relevant journal articles and book chapters.

Alerts

Create custom alerts tailored to a specific field of research or study.

Reading History

Keep track of the 100 most recently viewed articles or book chapters, sorted by date and time. Customize a view by deleting items or downloading the full list of entries.

Reading Recommendations

Get suggestions on what to read next. Combines Reading History with articles others have read and Scopus citation data to recommend other relevant information.

Integrations

See integrated chemical and structural data from Reaxys on chemistry content. Get citation data from Scopus on ScienceDirect article pages.
